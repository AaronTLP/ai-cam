Partnerships

AI@Cam’s mission is to drive a new wave of AI innovation that serves science, citizens, and society.

Academic & state partners

Baden-Württemberg is home to world-class research institutions in the field of artificial intelligence (AI). On the initiative of the Max Planck Society with funding from the state of Baden-Württemberg, several partners have come together to create a top location for research and innovation in AI. The participating academic institutions are the Universities of Stuttgart and Tübingen, the Fraunhofer-Gesellschaft and the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems.
